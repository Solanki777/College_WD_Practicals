About Library

Central library of Shantilal Shah Engineering College, Bhavnagar offers adequate facilities and good quality resources to both, students as well as faculties of the institute.

Library Hours

Library is open from 10:30 AM to 06:10 PM on all government working days,

  1. Submission of Requisition Slips : 11:00 AM to 02:00 PM
  2. Issue of Books Requisitioned on the same day : 02:00 PM to 04:00 PM

Library is committed to offer resources and services to all the students and faculties of the institute in a best possible manner.
